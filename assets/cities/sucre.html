Places of interest

Boat trip from Ajaccio to Capo d’Orto


Ajaccio looks dazzling when viewed from the bay. Boats leave Port Tino Rossi daily in summer for trips along the Rive Sud to Capo di Muro, with a stop for coffee and a swim en route.

The white cliffs of Bonifacio


The ferry ride from Bonifacio to Santa Teresa di Gallura in the north of Sardinia is thrilling, affording superb views of the fabled white cliffs of Bonifacio.

Kiosk selling tickets for boat trips to Plage du Loto


You’ll never forget the first time you set eyes on beautiful Plage du Loto. A rolling 45-minute ride across the gulf from St-Florent, the cove’s brilliant turquoise waters remain hidden until you are almost upon them. Depart early and you’ll have time to walk to Saleccia.

The village of Girolata


A fleet of excursion boats chugs out of Porto’s marina during the summer, shuttling visitors out to the famous Réserve Naturelle de Scandola, with its soaring red cliffs. Boats stop for lunch at Girolata.
