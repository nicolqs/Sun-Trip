Restaurant Casa da Morna


Part-owned by Cape Verdean musician Tito Paris, this African restaurant is more upmarket than most. It’s warmly-painted, windowless but cool, and there’s live music most nights.

Kaombo Club


This big club in the nightlife area of Alcântara draws a younger crowd – mainly Angolan – dancing to Afro-techno and other music.

Restaurant Moamba


A basic restaurant serving flavoursome dishes of various African origins. The big draw is the signature dish moamba de galinha, a chicken stew. Moamba is a short walk from some of the other African places.

The view from Castelo


The view from under the umbrella pines on the Castle’s esplanade takes in Alfama, the Baixa, Bairro Alto on the hill opposite, and the river. The light here is particularly warm in the late afternoon, encouraging you to linger.
