Places of interest

Sydney Cricket Ground


This area was granted to the British Army in 1851 (see Sydney Cricket Ground). In 1870 Rugby Union was first played here. Today, the hallowed turf hosts cricket in the summer, Australian Rules Football in the winter, and various sports and concerts in between.

Opera House


Experience the energy and passion of the national opera company when the young singers and international stars of Opera Australia perform throughout the year at the Sydney Opera House.

Sydney Theatre Company poster


Performing regularly at the Sydney Theatre & Walsh Bay, Sydney’s premier theatre companies are also in residence at the Sydney Opera House as well as performing at their home theatre at the Wharf under the Sydney Harbour Bridge. The STC presents an exciting mix of classics and new Australian drama.
