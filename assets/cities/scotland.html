Places of interest

Whale skull, Natural History gallery


Dinosaur skeletons and stuffed mammals cascade down from the ceiling producing spectacular visual results that almost bring them to life.

Scottish soldiers


Of the two world wars, it was the 1914–18 war that claimed the most lives: 74,000 Scottish soldiers and almost as many civilians. In addition to this, between 1901 and 1961, 1.4 million Scots emigrated to seek better lives elsewhere.

Scottish Parliament


In a 1997 referendum, the Scots emphatically voted for the re-establishment of a Scottish Parliament. This opened in 1999, returning the political forum to the heart of Scotland after an absence of 292 years.

Monet’s Poplars on the Epte


Of the two Monets, A Seascape, Shipping by Moonlight is a rare palette knife and brush application, while Poplars on the Epte is vintage Monet. Degas’ A Group of Dancers also features.
